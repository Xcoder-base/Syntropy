  • Modularity
MoE Routing Function:
$$y = \sum_{i=1}^{n} G(x)_i \cdot E_i(x)$$
Where \(G(x)\) is the gating network and \(E_i(x)\) are expert networks

Federated Learning with Secure Aggregation

  • Solves Data Bottleneck

Secure Aggregation:
$$\theta_{global} = \sum_{i=1}^{N} \frac{|D_i|}{|D|} \theta_i + \mathcal{N}(0, \sigma^2)$$

Proof-of-Learning (PoL) Consensus

  • Trust
  • Anti-sabotage
  • Incentives
PoL Verification:
$$V(\theta, \pi) = \begin{cases} 1 & \text{if } \mathcal{L}(\theta) \leq \mathcal{L}(\theta_{prev}) \cdot (1 - \epsilon) \\ 0 & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$$
Proof π validates improvement ε in loss function ℒ

$$P(i) = \frac{\exp(\alpha \cdot s_i)}{\sum_{j=1}^{k} \exp(\alpha \cdot s_j)}$$
